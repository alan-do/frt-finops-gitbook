# Introduction to FRT FinOps

Welcome to the official documentation for the **FinOps** project at **FPT Retail (FRT)** Technology Division.

This system is built to help the engineering, product, and management teams track, optimize, and manage technology-related costs (especially Cloud and AI/LLM costs) in an effective, automated, and transparent manner.

## 🎯 Our Mission

> "Bring financial accountability to the variable spend model of cloud and AI, enabling distributed teams to make business trade-offs between speed, cost, and quality."

## 📚 What's in this documentation?

- **Project Overview:** Objectives, KPIs, and Stakeholders.
- **FinOps Framework:** Core principles and the Inform - Optimize - Operate phases.
- **Implementation Scope:** Specific domains we are managing.
- **Development Process:** How the FinOps team builds internal tools using the CTO's 6-step workflow.
