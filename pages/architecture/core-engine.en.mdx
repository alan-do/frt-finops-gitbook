## Why this scope

The core is the deterministic, vendor-agnostic heart of the platform:

- **Connector contract + Mock** — `fetchRaw()` + `mapToFocus()`. The FOCUS standard is the seam; real connectors are add-ons that implement the same contract.
- **ETL Normalizer** — vendor columns → FOCUS 1.4, pure and traceable.
- **Data Quality (fail-closed)** — freshness, completeness, and per-currency zero-sum consistency, all equally blocking. No partial loads.
- **Allocation & Rules Engine** — priority-ordered (tag → account → shared-cost), largest-remainder split (exact penny allocation), `__UNALLOCATED__` bucket for idle/orphan cost, per-currency zero-sum verification. No AI in the calculation.
- **Versioned persistence** — append-only ingestion versions + Allocation_Run supersede-on-rerun for safe restatement.

The engine runs entirely on in-memory repositories, so none of the deferred infrastructure (Kafka, Temporal, ClickHouse, Postgres) is required to develop, test, or prove correctness. The swap to real stores is a token re-binding with no core logic change.
